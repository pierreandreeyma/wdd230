Upper Darby Chamber of Commerce

Overview

Location:
Upper Darby, Pennsylvania. USA
Site name:
Upper Darby Chamber Of Commerce

Purpose

1. Promote local businesses:

  • Directory: Provide a comprehensive directory of businesses located in Upper Darby, categorized by industry, making it easier for customers to find what they need and support local businesses.
  • Events and promotions: Showcase upcoming events, promotions, and special offers organized by businesses or the Chamber itself, attracting customers and increasing local economic activity.
  • Member benefits: Highlight the benefits of being a Chamber member, encouraging businesses to join and participate in Chamber initiatives.

2. Attract new businesses and residents:

  • Community information: Provide detailed information about Upper Darby, including its demographics, amenities, educational institutions, and cultural attractions, showcasing the township as an attractive place to live and do business.
  • Economic development: Highlight Upper Darby's business-friendly environment, tax incentives, and available resources for entrepreneurs, attracting potential investors and business owners.
  • Job listings: Offer a central platform for businesses to post job openings, connecting job seekers with local opportunities.

In summary, the Chamber of Commerce website for Upper Darby Township would serve as a valuable digital resource for local businesses, residents, and potential investors by promoting economic development, fostering communication, and encouraging collaboration within the community.

Audience

The Chamber of Commerce website for Upper Darby Township would have a diverse audience encompassing various groups with different interests and needs:

  • Local businesses: This includes existing and potential businesses within Upper Darby Township.
  • Residents of Upper Darby Township: This includes individuals and families living in the township.
  • Investors and potential residents: This includes individuals and organizations considering relocating to or investing in Upper Darby.

Scenarios

1. Scenario: Local Business Owner

  • Who: Sarah Jones, owner of Blooming Hearts Floral Boutique
  • Question: I'm a new business owner in Upper Darby and looking for ways to get involved in the community. What resources and events does the Chamber offer to help me connect with other businesses and potential customers?

This scenario represents a primary audience member, a local business owner seeking information about membership benefits and community engagement opportunities. The website content would address this by providing clear information.

2. Scenario: Resident of Upper Darby Township

  • Who: John Smith, resident of Upper Darby for 10 years
  • Question: My family and I are looking for fun things to do in the area this weekend. Are there any upcoming community events or festivals listed anywhere?

This scenario represents a secondary audience member, a resident seeking information about local events and activities. The website content would address this by providing easily accessible information.

3. Scenario: Potential Investor

  • Who: Maria Garcia, considering relocating her business to Upper Darby
  • Question: I'm interested in learning more about the business environment in Upper Darby. What resources or information can you provide about the local economy, demographics, and available business incentives?

This scenario represents a tertiary audience member, a potential investor seeking information about the business climate. The website content would address this providing imformation and resources.
